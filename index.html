Conclusion

THE ANSWER

It is not the position itself that determines aging resilience — it is the underlying skill profile. Players who improve or maintain their shooting efficiency (TS%) with age tend to age well regardless of position. PGs age well not because they are guards, but because their skill set — shooting and playmaking — is less dependent on physical decline.
